ABOUT THE COMPANY

We are a group of listenHers who are committed to discovering new female and non-binary aritists across the globe and sharing their unique sound with everyone. ListenHers was formed in 2022 by Laura, Priyana, Vanessa, and Vithuja. Together they felt that with their various musical backgrounds and shared passion for listening and reviewing all styles of music they could create a resource to all music enthusiasts.

We recognize talent.

Zaz

Zaz, whose real name is Isabelle Geffroy, is a French singer-songwriter who mixes jazzy styles, French variety, soul, and acoustic. Her songs are so catchy you’ll find yourself learning French as you sing along. Zaz’s album titled Paris makes you feel like you’re right in Paris with her. This album has so many amazing songs so it was really hard for our team to pick one favourite but our pick would have to be Champs-Elysées. This song is so jazzy and upbeat you can’t help but smile and dance to this masterpiece.

Kelsey Lu

Kelsey Lu is both a talented singer and talented cellist. Her music is classical while also modern at the same time, a mix of grounded and otherworldly. The perfect music to both jam out and relax to. She is based in Los Angeles, California. Kelsey Lu’s album titled Blood let’s your mind drift away as you hear these otherworldly amazing creations. Each song is so unique and beautiful so definitely a struggle to choose a favourite, but I’m Not In Love is phenomenal so that’s our pick. This song is so calming yet eerie. A listenHer favorite album for sure!
